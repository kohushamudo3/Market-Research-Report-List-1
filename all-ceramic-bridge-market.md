<p><ul><li>Traditional</li><li>Cantilever</li><li>Maryland</li><li>Implant-supported</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The all-ceramic bridge market includes various types designed to meet specific clinical needs. Traditional bridges are anchored by adjacent teeth, providing stability. Cantilever bridges extend from one anchor tooth, suitable for limited spaces. Maryland bridges use a clad framework that adheres to adjacent teeth, offering a conservative approach for replacement with minimal invasive techniques. Implant-supported bridges rely on dental implants for support, providing a durable option for tooth replacement, especially in cases where tooth loss has occurred.</p></p>
