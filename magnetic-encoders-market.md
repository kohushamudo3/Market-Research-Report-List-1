<p><p>Magnetic encoders are essential devices that convert position or motion into an electrical signal using magnetic sensors. The market is primarily divided into linear and rotary magnetic encoders. Linear magnetic encoders measure straight-line movement, providing precise position feedback along a linear path, crucial for applications like CNC machines and robotics. In contrast, rotary magnetic encoders assess rotational position or speed, making them vital for motor control and automation, commonly used in robotics, automotive, and industrial machinery.</p></p>
