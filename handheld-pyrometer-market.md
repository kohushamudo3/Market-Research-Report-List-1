<p><p>The handheld pyrometer market is primarily divided into two types: infrared and optical pyrometers. Infrared pyrometers detect thermal radiation emitted by objects to measure temperature without contact, making them ideal for various industrial applications. Optical pyrometers, on the other hand, rely on visible light to determine temperature, often used for higher temperature measurements in environments where infrared may be less effective. Both types offer portable, efficient solutions for temperature monitoring in diverse applications across different industries.</p></p>
