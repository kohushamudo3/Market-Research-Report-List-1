<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Tablet Coating Equipment Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Standard Coating Pan</li><li>Perforated Coating Pan</li><li>Fluidized Bed or Air Suspension Coater</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Tablet coating equipment encompasses various technologies designed to apply a protective layer or functional coating to tablets. Standard coating pans utilize gravity and rotation to evenly distribute the coating solution. Perforated coating pans allow for improved airflow and faster drying by using perforations in the pan. Fluidized bed or air suspension coaters employ a stream of air to suspend tablets, providing a uniform coating while efficiently managing temperature and moisture levels. Each type enhances tablet stability, appearance, and release properties.</p></p>
