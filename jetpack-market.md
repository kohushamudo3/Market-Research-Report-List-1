<p><p>The Jetpack market features two distinct types: Hydrogen Peroxide Driven and Fuel Driven. Hydrogen Peroxide Driven jetpacks utilize a chemical reaction between hydrogen peroxide and a catalyst to produce thrust, offering a cleaner, more efficient propulsion method. In contrast, Fuel Driven jetpacks rely on traditional fuels, such as gasoline, generating power through internal combustion engines. While the Fuel Driven market benefits from existing infrastructure and refueling techniques, Hydrogen Peroxide Driven systems present advantages in sustainability and reduced emissions, appealing to eco-conscious consumers.</p></p>
