<p><strong>The Pad Printing Inks Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Solvent-based</li><li>UV Curing</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The pad printing inks market is primarily divided into solvent-based and UV curing inks. Solvent-based inks use organic solvents as carriers, allowing for quick drying and effective adhesion to various surfaces, making them suitable for diverse applications. In contrast, UV curing inks utilize ultraviolet light to instantly cure and harden the ink upon exposure, offering advantages like enhanced durability and vibrant colors. Both types cater to different printing needs, balancing performance, environmental considerations, and application requirements.</p></p>
