<p><p>The high tensile bolts market comprises several types, each serving specific applications. Hexagon bolts are characterized by their six-sided heads, ideal for heavy torque applications. Button head bolts feature a rounded top, making them suitable for low-profile installations. Coach bolts are designed with a square neck to resist rotation, commonly used in timber connections. Plow bolts have a flat head and are used in heavy equipment. Other market types include specialized fasteners tailored for unique industrial needs, enhancing versatility in fastening solutions.</p></p>
