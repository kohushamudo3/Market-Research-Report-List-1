<p><p>The horticulture lighting market features three primary types: Fluorescent Lamps, High-Intensity Discharge (HID) Lights, and LED Lights. Fluorescent lamps are energy-efficient and suitable for small-scale applications, providing good light for plant growth. HID lights, including Metal Halide and High-Pressure Sodium, are powerful and ideal for larger setups, offering intense illumination but consuming more energy. LED lights are the most advanced option, offering customizable spectrums, high energy efficiency, and longevity, making them increasingly popular in commercial and residential horticulture.</p></p>
