<p><p>The automotive high-pressure sensor market is categorized mainly into two types: analog and digital sensors. In contrast, digital sensors convert pressure readings into digital signals, facilitating advanced data processing and integration with modern vehicle systems. Both types are essential for ensuring optimal engine performance, fuel efficiency, and safety, catering to the growing demand for precision in automotive applications.</p></p>
