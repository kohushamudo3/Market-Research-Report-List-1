<p><p>The Communication-Based Train Control (CBTC) system market includes various types based on different communication technologies. </p><p>1. **GSM-R**: Utilizes Global System for Mobile Communications for Railways, leveraging cellular networks for train communication.</p><p>2. **WLAN**: Employs Wireless Local Area Networks, ensuring high-speed data transfer between trains and control centers.</p><p>3. **Leaky Coaxial Cable**: Uses specially designed cables to facilitate reliable communication in tunnels or areas with limited signal strength.</p><p>4. **Fracture Waveguide**: Involves innovative techniques for wave propagation to enhance signal transmission.</p><p>5. **Tetra**: Based on Terrestrial Trunked Radio, providing robust communication through a dedicated radio network for railways.</p></p>
