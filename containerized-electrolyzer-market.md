<p><ul><li>Traditional Alkaline Electrolyzer</li><li>PEM Electrolyzer</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The containerized electrolyzer market features two primary types: traditional alkaline electrolyzers and PEM (Proton Exchange Membrane) electrolyzers. Traditional alkaline electrolyzers utilize a liquid alkaline electrolyte for efficient hydrogen production, often favored for large-scale applications due to their cost-effectiveness. In contrast, PEM electrolyzers use a solid polymer membrane, offering advantages in efficiency and responsiveness, making them suitable for dynamic renewable energy integration. Both types support the transition to cleaner energy by enabling on-site, scalable hydrogen generation in containerized setups.</p></p>
