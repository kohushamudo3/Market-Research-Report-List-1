<p><p>The aerospace landing gears market is primarily divided into two main types: main landing gear and nose/tail landing gear. The main landing gear supports the aircraft's weight during landing and takeoff, providing stability and strength. In contrast, nose landing gear is located at the front of the aircraft, aiding in directional control and shock absorption, while tail landing gear is used in certain aircraft designs for balance and support. Each type plays a crucial role in overall aircraft performance and safety.</p></p>
