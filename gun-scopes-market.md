<p><p>The gun scopes market comprises two main types: optical and mechanical. Optical scopes utilize lenses and prisms to magnify the target image, enhancing accuracy in shooting. They often feature reticles and adjustable magnification, catering to varying distances. In contrast, mechanical scopes rely on physical components, such as iron sights or reflex designs, to assist shooters without magnification. Both types serve distinct preferences and needs within the shooting community, ranging from hunting to tactical applications.</p></p>
