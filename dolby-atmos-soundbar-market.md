<p><p>The Dolby Atmos soundbar market features two primary types: upward-firing soundbars and virtualized soundbars. Upward-firing soundbars utilize specialized drivers that project sound upwards, creating a three-dimensional audio experience by bouncing sound off the ceiling. This enhances immersion, especially for overhead sound effects. In contrast, virtualized soundbars simulate surround sound using advanced processing techniques, delivering a more expansive audio field without the need for extra speakers. Both types aim to elevate the home audio experience, making it accessible and engaging.</p></p>
