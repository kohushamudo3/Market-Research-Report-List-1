<p><p>The optical modulators market consists of three main types: </p><p>1. **Intensity Modulators**: These devices control the amplitude of light waves, enabling effective data transmission by varying light intensity.</p><p>2. **Phase Modulators**: These modulators adjust the phase of light waves without changing their amplitude, optimizing signal fidelity and supporting advanced communication systems.</p><p>3. **Spatial Light Modulators**: These devices manipulate light in two dimensions, allowing for dynamic control of light patterns, crucial in applications like display technology and optical computing.</p></p>
