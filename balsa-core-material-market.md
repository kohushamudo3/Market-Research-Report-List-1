<p><p>The Balsa Core Material market is typically categorized into two types: monolayer and multilayer. Monolayer balsa core consists of a single sheet of balsa wood, offering lightweight properties and structural integrity for various applications. In contrast, multilayer balsa core features multiple layers of balsa, enhancing strength and durability while maintaining low weight. This multilayer design allows for improved performance in demanding environments, making it suitable for advanced composite industries such as aerospace, marine, and automotive sectors.</p></p>
