<p><p>The cartilage repair and regeneration market is categorized into two main types: cell-based and non-cell-based therapies. Cell-based therapies involve techniques like stem cell injections or tissue engineering, utilizing living cells to promote cartilage healing and regeneration. In contrast, non-cell-based therapies focus on synthetic or biological materials, such as scaffolds or gels, that support cartilage repair without the use of living cells. Both approaches aim to restore cartilage function and alleviate pain, catering to various patient needs in orthopedic treatments.</p></p>
