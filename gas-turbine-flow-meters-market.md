<p><p>Gas turbine flow meters are classified into several types based on their design and functionality. Axial type meters measure flow by detecting the rotation of turbine blades aligned with the flow direction, offering high accuracy in high-speed applications. Tangential type meters capture flow from a side angle, effectively measuring lower flow rates. Mechanical type meters utilize moving parts to gauge flow, suitable for various conditions. Other types may include specialized designs for unique applications, expanding the market's versatility.</p></p>
