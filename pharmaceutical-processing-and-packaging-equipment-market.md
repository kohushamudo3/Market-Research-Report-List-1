<p><p>The pharmaceutical processing and packaging equipment market encompasses various essential types that facilitate drug production. Centrifuges separate substances based on density, crucial for purifying compounds. Dryers remove moisture from products, ensuring stability, and granulators combine powders into granules for efficient processing. Other equipment includes filling machines, labeling devices, and quality control systems, collectively ensuring effective manufacturing.</p></p>
