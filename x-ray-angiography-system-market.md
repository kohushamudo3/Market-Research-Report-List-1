<p><p>The X-Ray Angiography System market is segmented into various types based on the anatomical regions they target. Head part angiography focuses on visualizing cerebral and vascular structures in the brain, aiding in diagnosing strokes and aneurysms. Chest angiography assesses the pulmonary and cardiac vascular systems for conditions like pulmonary embolism. Abdominal angiography examines blood vessels in the abdominal region, helping diagnose organ-related diseases. Heart angiography, or coronary angiography, is crucial for assessing coronary arteries and diagnosing heart conditions.</p></p>
