<p><ul><li>TTL Loupes</li><li>Flip-up Loupes</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The medical binocular loupe market consists primarily of two types: TTL (Through-The-Lens) loupes and flip-up loupes. TTL loupes are custom-made and mounted directly to the prescription lenses, providing a streamlined design and optimal comfort. In contrast, flip-up loupes attach to the glasses and can be easily flipped up when not in use, offering flexibility during procedures. Both types enhance magnification and visualization, essential for precision in fields such as dentistry and surgery, improving overall patient outcomes.</p></p>
